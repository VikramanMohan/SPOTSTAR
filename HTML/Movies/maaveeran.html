Maaveeran

156 minutes Action 2023 IMDB 8.5/10
The story follows Sathya, a timid cartoonist who creates a comic strip about a brave warrior named Maaveeran. After an accident, Sathya starts hearing a voice that guides him to confront a corrupt politician, Jeyakodi. With the help of this mysterious voice, Sathya transforms from a coward into a courageous hero, fighting against corruption and injustice12.
