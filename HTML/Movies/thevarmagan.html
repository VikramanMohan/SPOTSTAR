ThevarMagan

178 minutes Drama 1992 IMDB 8.7/10

The film follows Sakthivel Thevar, who returns to his village after completing his education in London. He brings his westernized girlfriend, Bhanumathi, which displeases his father, Periyasamy Thevar, a respected village chieftain. Sakthivel plans to open a chain of restaurants, but his father wants him to help the villagers. The village is plagued by a long-standing feud between Periyasamy Thevar and his half-brother Chinnasamy Thevar, and nephew Maya Thevar. As tensions escalate, Sakthivel is drawn into the conflict, leading to dramatic and violent confrontations.
