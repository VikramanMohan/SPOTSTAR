The Social Network

156 minutes Biography 2010 IMDB 7.8/10

The Social Network chronicles the creation of Facebook and the legal battles that followed. It focuses on Mark Zuckerberg (Jesse Eisenberg) as he navigates the complexities of friendship, betrayal, and ambition in the tech world. The film is known for its sharp dialogue, crafted by Aaron Sorkin, and its exploration of the impact of social media.
