Avatar

186 minutes Sci-Fi 2009 IMDB 8/10

Jake Sully, a paraplegic former Marine, replaces his deceased identical twin brother as an operator of an Avatar—a genetically engineered Na'vi-human hybrid—so he can infiltrate the tribe. As Jake integrates into Na'vi society and falls in love with Neytiri, one of the tribe members, he finds himself torn between following orders from his human superiors and protecting the Na'vi and their home. The film explores themes of environmental conservation, imperialism, and the clash of cultures.
