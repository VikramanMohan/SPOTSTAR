Thani Oruvan

166 minutes Thriller 2015 IMDB 8.5/10
The film follows Mithran, a dedicated IPS officer, who is on a mission to expose and bring down Siddharth Abimanyu, a corrupt scientist involved in illegal medical practices. Mithran’s journey is filled with intense challenges as he navigates through Siddharth’s cunning tactics and powerful influence.
