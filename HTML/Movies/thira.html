Thira

128 minutes Thriller 2013 IMDB 8/10

This Film is a gripping thriller that revolves around Dr. Rohini Pranab, a cardiac surgeon and social activist who is on a mission to expose human trafficking. When her younger sister is kidnapped, Rohini teams up with Naveen, a young man searching for his missing sister, to bring down the trafficking ring. The movie is fast-paced, with intense performances and a strong message against the exploitation of women and children. It is the first installment in a planned trilogy, combining elements of suspense, drama, and social commentary.
