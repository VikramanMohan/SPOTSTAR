Jerry Maguire

176 minutes Horror 1996 IMDB 7.5/10

This Film is a romantic dramedy about a sports agent, Jerry Maguire (Tom Cruise), who experiences a crisis of conscience and decides to write a mission statement that leads to his firing. Striking out on his own with just one client, Rod Tidwell (Cuba Gooding Jr.), and his loyal assistant, Dorothy Boyd (Renée Zellweger), Jerry must rebuild his career and find personal fulfillment. The film is known for its memorable lines, including "Show me the money!" and "You complete me."
