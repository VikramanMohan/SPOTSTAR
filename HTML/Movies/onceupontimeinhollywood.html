Once Upon Time in Hollywood

178 minutes Drama 2019 IMDB 7.9/10

Set in 1969 Los Angeles, the film follows Rick Dalton (played by Leonardo DiCaprio), a fading television actor, and his stunt double, Cliff Booth (played by Brad Pitt), as they navigate the rapidly changing film industry. Rick struggles to revive his career while Cliff remains his loyal friend and confidant. Their lives intersect with several real-life figures, including actress Sharon Tate (played by Margot Robbie), who lives next door to Rick
