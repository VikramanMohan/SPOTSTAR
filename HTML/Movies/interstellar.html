Interstellar

176 minutes Sci-Fi 2014 IMDB 9.7/10

This Film is an epic science fiction film set in a dystopian future where Earth is becoming uninhabitable. The story follows former pilot Cooper (Matthew McConaughey) as he leads a team of astronauts, including Dr. Amelia Brand (Anne Hathaway), on a mission through a wormhole in search of a new habitable planet for humanity. The film explores themes of love, sacrifice, time, and space, featuring stunning visuals and a deeply emotional narrative. The relationship between Cooper and his daughter, Murph, plays a central role in the film's emotional arc.
