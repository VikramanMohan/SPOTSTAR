Edge of Tomorrow

176 minutes Sci-Fi 2014 IMDB 7.7/10

This Film is a sci-fi action film set in a future where Earth is under attack by an alien race. Major William Cage (Tom Cruise), a military officer with no combat experience, is forced into a suicide mission, where he discovers that he's caught in a time loop. Each time he dies, he relives the same day, gaining valuable combat skills with the help of war hero Rita Vrataski (Emily Blunt). Together, they must find a way to defeat the aliens and break the loop. The film is known for its inventive premise, gripping action, and the chemistry between Cruise and Blunt.
