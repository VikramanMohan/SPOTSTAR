Mayaanadhi

146 minutes Drama 2017 IMDB 7.8/10
This Film is a romantic drama that tells the intense love story of Maathan, a small-time criminal on the run, and Aparna, an aspiring actress. The film explores their complex relationship, which is marked by passion, trust issues, and the consequences of Maathan’s criminal past catching up with them. It’s noted for its realistic portrayal of love and the strong performances by the lead actors.

Cast

Tovino Thomas, Aishwaraya Lakshmi

Director

Aashiq Abu
