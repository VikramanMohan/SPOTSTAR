Meiyazhagan

176 minutes Drama 2024 IMDB 9.6/10
The film follows the emotional journey of Arulmozhi Varman (Arul), who returns to his ancestral home in Thanjavur after twenty-two years for his cousin Bhuvaneshwari's wedding. The visit stirs up deep nostalgia and unresolved emotions as Arul reconnects with family and friends, including Sudalamuthu (Sokku mama) and Latha, his cousin with a troubled marriage. The film delves into themes of family bonds, the passage of time, and the enduring impact of memories. Through Arul's visit, the story beautifully captures the essence of revisiting one's roots and the bittersweet nature of familial relationships.
