Ee Ma Yu(R.I.P)

176 minutes Drama 2018 IMDB 8/10

Set in the coastal village of Chellanam in Ernakulam, the film revolves around the death and funeral of Vavachan Mesthiri. After returning from a long journey, Vavachan gets into a fight, and events unfold between two evenings. His son, Eeshi, promises him a grand funeral. As the community gathers to mourn, secrets, perspectives, and different reactions to death come to the forefront. The film beautifully captures the nuances of grief, tradition, and human nature
