CATCH ME IF YOU CAN

176 minutes Biography 2002 IMDB 8.1/10

This Film is based on the true story of Frank Abagnale Jr. (Leonardo DiCaprio), a young con artist who successfully poses as a pilot, doctor, and lawyer while forging millions of dollars in checks. FBI agent Carl Hanratty (Tom Hanks) becomes obsessed with capturing Frank, leading to a cat-and-mouse chase across the globe. The film blends drama with humor and offers a fascinating look at deception and pursuit.
