Machinist

146 minutes Thriller 2004 IMDB 7.5/10

Trevor Reznik, a machinist, suffers from severe insomnia, leaving him emaciated and on the brink of paranoia. His coworkers shun him due to his appearance and odd behavior. When an accident occurs at work, Trevor is blamed, especially after being distracted by an unfamiliar coworker named Ivan—a mysterious figure with no records. As Trevor grapples with recurring imagery and cryptic post-it notes, he suspects a concerted effort to drive him insane. Relationships with Stevie (a caring prostitute) and Maria (a waitress) add layers of complexity. The film weaves a web of intrigue and psychological tension.
