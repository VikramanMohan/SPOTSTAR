Prisoners

166 minutes Thriller 2013 IMDB 8.6/10
This Movie is a tense thriller about the abduction of two young girls in a small town. Keller Dover (Hugh Jackman), the father of one of the girls, takes matters into his own hands when he believes the police, led by Detective Loki (Jake Gyllenhaal), aren't doing enough to find them. As the investigation stalls, Dover's desperation leads him to take extreme measures to find his daughter, pushing the boundaries of morality and justice. The film is a gripping exploration of fear, faith, and the lengths a parent will go to protect their child.
