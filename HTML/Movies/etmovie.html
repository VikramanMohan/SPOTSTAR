E.T. THE EXTRA-TERRESTRIAL

166 minutes Sci-Fi 1982 IMDB 9/10

This Film is a heartwarming sci-fi film about a young boy named Elliott (Henry Thomas) who befriends an alien stranded on Earth. As Elliott and his siblings try to help E.T. return home, they must evade government agents who want to capture the alien. The film is celebrated for its emotional depth, iconic score by John Williams, and its universal themes of friendship and home.
