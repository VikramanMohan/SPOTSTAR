ManjummalBoys

136 minutes Drama 2024 IMDB 8.6/10
This Film is a comedy-drama that revolves around a group of friends from the small town of Manjummel. The film captures their day-to-day lives, filled with humor, camaraderie, and the challenges they face as they navigate love, work, and personal dreams. The story emphasizes the bond of friendship and how these boys stick together through thick and thin, dealing with life's ups and downs in a light-hearted and entertaining way.
