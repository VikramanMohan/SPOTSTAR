Kadaisi Vivasayi

176 minutes Drama 2021 IMDB 9.6/10
The film follows Mayandi, an 85-year-old farmer who is the last active farmer in his village. He lives a simple, frugal life and is tasked with providing the symbolic first grain for the village temple’s annual ceremony. However, his life takes a dramatic turn when he is falsely accused of killing peacocks, India’s national bird. The story unfolds as Mayandi navigates the legal system and the challenges of maintaining his farm while in jail
