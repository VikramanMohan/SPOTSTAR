Taramani

168 minutes Drama 2017 IMDB 8/10

This Film is a contemporary drama that explores the complexities of modern relationships through the lives of Althea, an independent single mother, and Prabhunath, a traditional man struggling with his insecurities. The film tackles themes like gender roles, sexuality, and the challenges of living in an urban, globalized society, set against the backdrop of Chennai's Taramani area.
