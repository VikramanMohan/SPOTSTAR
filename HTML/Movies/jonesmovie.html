INDIANA JONES AND THE RAIDERS OF THE LOST ARK

156 minutes Adventure 1981 IMDB 8.2/10

Set in 1936, the story follows the intrepid archaeologist Indiana Jones (played by Harrison Ford). The U.S. government hires him to find the Ark of the Covenant before the Nazis can get their hands on it. The Ark is believed to hold extraordinary powers, and Indy embarks on a globetrotting adventure to prevent its misuse. Along the way, he faces danger, ancient traps, and formidable foes, including the cunning archaeologist Belloq (played by Paul Freeman). The film is filled with thrilling action sequences, memorable characters, and a dash of humor.
