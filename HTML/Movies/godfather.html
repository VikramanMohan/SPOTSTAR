The Godfather Part 1

126 minutes Crime 1972 IMDB 9.6/10
The Godfather is the story of the powerful Italian-American crime family of Don Vito Corleone (Marlon Brando). As he ages, he hands control of his empire to his youngest son, Michael Corleone (Al Pacino), who initially resists but eventually embraces the life of crime and power. The film explores themes of loyalty, power, and corruption within the family and the larger criminal world.
