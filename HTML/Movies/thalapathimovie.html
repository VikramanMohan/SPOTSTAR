Thalapathi

170 minutes Drama 1991 IMDB 8.8/10
The story revolves around Surya (Rajinikanth), an orphan raised in a slum, who befriends Devaraj (Mammootty), a powerful gangster. Their bond is tested when a new district collector, Arjun (Arvind Swamy), arrives with a mission to end violence. The plot is inspired by the friendship between Karna and Duryodhana from the Hindu epic, Mahabharata
