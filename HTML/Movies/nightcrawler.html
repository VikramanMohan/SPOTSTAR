This Film follows Louis Bloom (Jake Gyllenhaal), a driven and morally ambiguous man who enters the world of freelance crime journalism in Los Angeles. Armed with a camera, he begins to film violent and gruesome incidents, selling the footage to local news stations. As Lou's ambition grows, he blurs the lines between observer and participant, leading to increasingly dangerous and unethical behavior. The film is a dark exploration of media sensationalism and the lengths people will go to for success.
