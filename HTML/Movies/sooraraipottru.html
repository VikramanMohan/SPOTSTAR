Soorarai pottru

170 minutes Biography 2020 IMDB 8.9/10

The film is inspired by the life of Captain G.R. Gopinath, the founder of Air Deccan. It follows the story of Nedumaaran Rajangam (Maara), who dreams of launching a low-cost airline to make air travel affordable for everyone in India. The movie depicts his struggles against a hostile aviation industry and his determination to fulfill his dream.
