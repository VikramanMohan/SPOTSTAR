196 minutes Crime 1995 IMDB 7.9/10

As he oversees the Tangiers Casino, Ace becomes entangled in a world of gambling, corruption, and violence. His relationship with his ambitious and troubled wife, Ginger, further complicates his life as she becomes involved with a dangerous mob associate.
