Kill Bill VOL.1

176 minutes Action 2003 IMDB 8.9/10

The story follows “The Bride” (played by Uma Thurman), a former member of the Deadly Viper Assassination Squad. On the day of her wedding rehearsal, her ex-boss and lover, Bill (played by David Carradine), along with the Deadly Vipers, brutally attack her, leaving her in a coma. Four years later, she awakens and discovers that her unborn child is gone. Fueled by a desire for revenge, she sets out to kill Bill and the other members of the squad
