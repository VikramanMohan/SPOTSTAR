Vaaranam Aayiram

179 minutes Romance 2008 IMDB 8.6/10

The film revolves around Suriya, an Indian Army Major, who learns about his father Krishnan’s sudden death while on a rescue mission. As he grapples with grief, he reminisces about the bond he shared with his father. We’re taken back to the 1970s, where Krishnan and Malini, both college students, fall in love and eventually get married. Their journey unfolds through the lens of their son, Suriya. Love, responsibility, and family dynamics shape their lives. The film beautifully captures their moments—both heartwarming and heartbreaking—across time and continents
