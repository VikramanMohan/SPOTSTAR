7G Rainbow colony

190 minutes Romance 2004 IMDB 8/10
The story follows Kadhir, a wayward and irresponsible youth from a lower-middle-class family living in Rainbow Colony, Chennai. Kadhir is perceived as a good-for-nothing person who skips classes, fails exams, and gets into fights. His life takes a turn when a once well-off family moves into the same colony due to a business loss. Kadhir becomes infatuated with Anita, the daughter of the new neighbors.
