Vinnaithaandi Varuvaayaa

166 minutes Romance 2010 IMDB 8.6/10
The film explores the complicated relationship between Karthik, a Hindu Tamil boy, and Jessie, a Malayali Christian girl from Alappuzha, Kerala. Karthik falls deeply in love with Jessie, but their union faces immense challenges. Their different religions and Jessie’s strict, conservative family create barriers. Despite Karthik’s unwavering affection, Jessie remains indifferent and reluctant. The film delves into themes of love, faith, and societal norms, leaving viewers emotionally invested in their journey
