Angamaly Diaries

176 minutes Crime 2017 IMDB 8/10

This Film is a raw and gritty film that portrays the life of Vincent Pepe and his group of friends in Angamaly, Kerala. The story delves into their involvement in local gang wars, their aspirations of starting a pork business, and the chaotic, often violent events that follow. The film is renowned for its realistic portrayal of life in Angamaly and its climactic 11-minute single-take shot.
