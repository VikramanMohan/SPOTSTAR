Petta

170 minutes Action 2019 IMDB 7.5/10

The story follows Kaali, a mysterious and seemingly aged man who takes up the job of a boys’ hostel warden at a college in Ooty. Kaali quickly notices that a group of unruly final-year students, led by Michael, dominate the hostel. He puts a stop to Michael’s antics, leading to a rivalry between them. Kaali also forms a close bond with Anwar, an NRI student, and helps him with his relationship with Anu, the daughter of a pranic healer.
