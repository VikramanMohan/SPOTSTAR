Source Code

176 minutes Sci-Fi 2011 IMDB 7.5/10

This Flim is centers on Captain Colter Stevens (Jake Gyllenhaal), a soldier who wakes up in another man's body aboard a commuter train that is destined to explode in eight minutes.
