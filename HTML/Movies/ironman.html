Iron Man

166 minutes Sci-Fi 2008 IMDB 7.6/10
Iron Man introduces Tony Stark (Robert Downey Jr.), a wealthy industrialist and genius inventor who is captured by terrorists and forced to build a weapon of mass destruction. Instead, he creates a high-tech suit of armor to escape and later refines the suit to become Iron Man, a superhero dedicated to protecting the world. This film kickstarted the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) and is known for Downey Jr.'s charismatic performance.
