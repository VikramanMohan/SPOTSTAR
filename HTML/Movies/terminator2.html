Terminator 2: Judgment Day

176 minutes Sci-Fi 1991 IMDB 8.2/10

This Film is a landmark action and sci-fi film that follows Sarah Connor (Linda Hamilton) and her son John (Edward Furlong) as they are pursued by a new, advanced Terminator, the T-1000 (Robert Patrick). They are protected by the original T-800 Terminator (Arnold Schwarzenegger), now reprogrammed to be their guardian. The film is known for its revolutionary special effects, intense action sequences, and deeper exploration of themes like fate and humanity's relationship with technology.
