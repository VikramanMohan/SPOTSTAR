Marakkar: Arabikadalinte Simham

176 minutes Historical 2021 IMDB 8.2/10

This Film is an epic historical film that narrates the life of Kunjali Marakkar IV, the naval commander of the Zamorin of Calicut, who defended the Malabar Coast against Portuguese invasion in the 16th century. The film explores themes of valor, patriotism, and the fight against colonialism.
