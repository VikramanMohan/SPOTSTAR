Aliens

176 minutes Horror 1986 IMDB 7.5/10

Aliens is the action-packed sequel to Ridley Scott's Alien (1979), where Ellen Ripley (Sigourney Weaver) returns to the planet where her crew first encountered the alien species, this time with a group of space marines. As they investigate, they discover that the alien threat is far more deadly than they had anticipated. The film is lauded for its thrilling action, intense atmosphere, and Weaver's powerful performance as Ripley, solidifying the character as one of cinema's most iconic heroines.
