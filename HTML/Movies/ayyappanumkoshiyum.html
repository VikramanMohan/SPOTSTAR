Ayyappanum Koshiyum

178 minutes Action 2020 IMDB 8/10

It is a gripping action-drama that revolves around the intense conflict between Ayyappan Nair, a disciplined and dutiful police officer, and Koshy Kurian, a wealthy and influential ex-havildar. The movie explores themes of ego, power, and pride as the two strong-willed men clash over a seemingly trivial matter that escalates into a full-blown feud, with each trying to outdo the other. The film is praised for its powerful performances, especially by Prithviraj Sukumaran and Biju Menon in the lead roles
