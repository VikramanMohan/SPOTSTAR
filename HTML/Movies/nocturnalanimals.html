Nocturnal Animals

146 minutes Thriller 2016 IMDB 7.5/10

that tells two intertwined stories. The first follows Susan Morrow (Amy Adams), an art gallery owner who receives a manuscript from her ex-husband, Edward Sheffield (Jake Gyllenhaal). The manuscript is a dark and violent novel dedicated to her, which she interprets as a symbolic revenge for the way she ended their relationship. The second story is the plot of the novel itself, involving a man named Tony (also played by Gyllenhaal) who seeks vengeance after a brutal crime against his family. The film is a haunting exploration of regret, revenge, and the consequences of past decisions.
