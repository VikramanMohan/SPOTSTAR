Kammattipaadam

168 minutes Crime 2016 IMDB 8.5/10

It is a gritty crime drama that delves into the transformation of Kochi from a small village to a bustling metropolis, focusing on the life of Krishnan and his friends who get involved in the city's underworld. The film highlights issues like land mafia, urbanization, and the impact on the local Dalit community, with powerful performances and a realistic portrayal of violence and survival.
