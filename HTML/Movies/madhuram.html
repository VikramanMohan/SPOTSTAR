Madhuram

146 minutes Drama 2021 IMDB 8.6/10
This Film is a sweet and heartwarming drama that unfolds in the setting of a hospital, where a group of people waiting for their loved ones to recover develop deep bonds with each other. The film explores love, friendship, and the human connections that provide hope and solace during difficult times.
