THAMARAI

Thamarai, who has given a collection of poems called "Eru Doorum Kadu Lallipal" and can also write short stories. He has authored short story collections "Moonstones" and "Some Pages from the Middle of My Diary". He has received Tirupur Tamil Sangha Award, Sculptor Award and various other prizes for his literary works.
